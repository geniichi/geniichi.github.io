Calculus

Calculus is a type of math that helps us understand how things change over time or in response to varying inputs. It involves studying functions and their properties, and is used in many fields to help solve complex problems.

Chemistry

Chemistry is a branch of science that deals with the composition, properties, and reactions of matter. It involves studying the structure and behavior of atoms and molecules, and how they interact with each other to form new substances. Chemistry is used in many fields, including medicine, materials science, environmental science, and engineering, and is essential for understanding the world around us.

World History

World history is the study of past events, developments, and changes in human societies and civilizations around the world, from ancient times to the present day. It helps us understand how different regions and cultures have evolved over time, and the factors that have influenced them.

Biology

Biology is the study of living organisms and their interactions with each other and their environment. It helps us understand the structure, function, and behavior of living things, from the smallest cells to entire ecosystems.
